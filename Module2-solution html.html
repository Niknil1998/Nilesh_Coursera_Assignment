Momos

Momos is a Tibetan dish which is now popular all over the world. But in India, people have a different kind of obsession for Momos, especially among teenagers. Everyone, from almost every age group, just loves to have hot steamed momos with spicy red chutney

PuranPoli

The traditional Indian treat. This lentil stuffed sweet flatbread is a popular Maharashtrian recipe made during Ganesh Chaturthi, Diwali, Holi or any other festive occasion. the results are well worth it! And as an added bonus, this sweet recipe also happens to be pretty darn nutritious, meaning you can even eat it for breakfast.
